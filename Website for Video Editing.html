Good question. After writing your HTML and CSS, you need hosting. Thats where your files live so people can see your website online.

You have 3 easy options.

Option 1: Netlify (Free and easiest)

Best if youre just starting.

Steps:

  1. Go to netlify.com

  2. Create a free account (use Gmail)

  3. Click Add new site Deploy manually

  4. Drag and drop your whole website folder (the one with index.html and style.css)

  5. Done. Your site goes live instantly

Netlify will give you a random link like:
something.netlify.app

You can later connect your own domain.

Option 2: GitHub + Netlify (More professional)

  1. Create account on github.com

  2. Create new repository

  3. Upload your index.html and style.css

  4. Connect GitHub to Netlify

  5. Netlify will auto deploy

This is better if you plan to update your site often.

Option 3: Paid Hosting (Hostinger or Bluehost)

Best if you want your own domain like:
rseditingstudio.com

Steps:

  1. Buy hosting plan

  2. Buy domain

  3. Login to hosting dashboard

  4. Go to File Manager

  5. Open public_html folder

  6. Upload index.html and style.css there

Once uploaded, your website will open from your domain.

If youre just testing, I suggest Netlify first. Its free and fast.
